Welcome to DSC 3163: Data Visualization 

This course is sequenced in order from one module to the next. It is recommended that you navigate through each module in order as a class, following the course schedule. Course materials include reading assignments, lectures or presentations, images, watching videos, writing assignments, discussion forums, and quizzes and tests. All activities will be submitted digitally. The primary modes of communication will be email, announcements, and discussion forums.
